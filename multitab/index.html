WHY << your business>>?

For WHY NOMINATE?

Having embarked on digital transformation journeys prior to and during the COVID-19 pandemic, the financial services industry vastly improved the usability of digital experiences in line with customer expectations. Taking cognizance of rapid disruptions across the landscape forced service providers to rethink current business models and adapt to a customer centric approach. The penetration of digital devices worldwide also forced banks to now build an effective and organic relationship with its customers. The benefits of these relationship are already reaping rewards as banks now have access to over a million data-points to enhance customer engagement. Yet, banks are under pressure from disruptors and new entrants who are actively growing in numbers and expanding their customer base.

Now, more than ever, it is imperative that financial service firms deliver exceptional digital experiences through innovative business models and services.
