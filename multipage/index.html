The << Site Name >> is an internal competition open to all Ecobankers to propose innovative ideas that will help continuously improve customer experience and grow the business well into the future. The concept of the race was inspired by our RACEIT values: Respect, Accountability, Customer Centricity, Excellence, Integrity, Teamwork.

Purpose

The 2022 << Site Name >> is aligned to our Execution Momentum phase and to our Core Values, in particular Excellence, Customer Centricity and Teamwork. The purpose of this contest is to drive innovation from within the Bank by motivating Ecobankers to share their ideas and be given an opportunity to bring them to life. The ultimate aim of the contest is to implement the best idea(s) across the Ecobank Group.

What ideas are we looking for?

Ideas should align with our Execution Momentum objectives. The main themes are in line with the three priorities defined during our Business Leadership Conference (BLC):

  • Winning in payments - increasing the payment wallet size
  • Become customer centric by using technology to better serve our customers
  • Increase revenue by driving the culture of salesmanship (strengthen the sales spirit amongst all Ecobankers)

The overall objective is to drive innovation, foster teamwork and deliver exceptional solutions for our customers.

Competition roll out

Anyone can submit her/his/their idea. From the entries our judges will select the 10 ﬁnalists (also called athletes).

If your idea is one of the ﬁnal 10, you will work with your team (the Bank will support you inbuilding your team if you don’t have one), to bring your project to life.

The teams will receive support in the initial incubation phase, including training on Design Thinking and mentors for support and to provide input. All staff will be invited to vote on the top 10 projects. The projects will then be presented to a senior jury to select the winner of the 2022 Ecobank Innovation Award.

Staff are invited to submit ideas with direct impact on the business (Increase payment Wallet, Increase profitability, using IT to better serve customers…).
All entries are evaluated. Staff will vote to top 25, and a committee will select the top 10

Teams present prototype and demonstrate value to be generated to the senior leadership on a demo day .
Sponsored projects will be considered for integration and commercialization.

The submission criteria

You must be an Ecobank Staff member - conﬁrmed, temporary, trainee from any Ecobank location ( Africa and International).

You can submit your idea individually or as a team. Your idea should serve one of these three business priorities:

  • Winning in payments - Increasing the payment wallet size
  • Become customer centric by using technology to better serve our customers
  • Increase revenue by driving the culture of salesmanship (strengthen the sales spirit amongst all Ecobankers)

Your idea can be a product, a process etc.

Phase 1

For the first phase, your idea will be assessed as follow:

  1. Innovation
  2. User Experience
  3. Business Opportunity
  4. Scalability
  5. Wow Effect

Winning ideas

The winning ideas amongst the top 10 finalists will be selected based on the following criteria:

  1. The market size – How to assess the potential size of the market opportunity for this solution?
  2. The market relevance – How relevant is the solution to pain points within the African context?
  3. The scalability – How scalable is the product for multiple markets?
  4. The Potential revenue – How big is the potential revenue?
  5. The Business Model – How do we rate the viability and sustainability of the business model?

After the official opening ceremony, the Heats will commence. You will have until 22nd September to submit your ideas.
